This is a cat !

The domestic cat is a small, typically furry, carnivorous mammal. They are often called house cats when kept as indoor pets or simply cats when there is no need to distinguish them from other felids and felines. Wikipedia Lifespan: 4 – 5 years (In the wild) Length: 46 cm (Without Tail) Gestation period: 64 – 67 days Scientific name: Felis catus Mass: 3.6 – 4.5 kg (Adult) Did you know: Cats that suffer prolonged or repeated bouts of respiratory infections should be checked for FeLV and FIV.
